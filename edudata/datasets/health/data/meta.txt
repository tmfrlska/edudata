The Korean Genome and Epidemiology Study (KoGES) is a large cohort study project designed to identify gene-environment factors and their interactions in common chronic diseases, such as type 2 diabetes, hypertension, metabolic syndrome, obesity, and cardiovascular disease in Korea. This datasets contains the epidemiological data on randomly sampled 1,000 Koreans. Each participant was tested and surveyed 5 times over the course of 15 years.

Data source: http://www.cdc.go.kr/contents.es?mid=a40504070100
